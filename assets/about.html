COVID-19 Analysis And Prediction Of Spreading Rate

About This Project

It's been over a year since the outbreak of COVID-19 has started. All countries are trying hard to control the spreading of virus. If you have over a billion people in a country and you are trying to stop a spreading of a virus which speads through air easily is very very difficult. Government has to think over all the different aspect before taking any decision. This project is a small step against the virus and help citizens of India to foresee the conditions and the governing bodies to take the decisions quickly. We have build a machine learning model trained on clinical data of COVID patients available and predicting the number of cases for next 7 days.
